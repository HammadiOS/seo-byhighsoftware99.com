Technical SEO Questions Answered

Common questions about technical SEO and what our audit covers.

How long does a technical SEO audit take?
A comprehensive audit typically takes 5–10 business days depending on site size. We deliver a prioritized report with clear fix recommendations and severity ratings.
Do you fix issues or just report them?
We both identify and implement fixes. With CMS access, we handle implementation directly. For server-level changes, we work alongside your development team with precise instructions.
My site was penalized — can you help?
Yes. We perform penalty recovery audits identifying both manual actions and algorithmic penalties, then execute a recovery strategy including content cleanup and link disavow where necessary.
Do you work with all CMS platforms?
Yes — WordPress, Shopify, Wix, Webflow, Squarespace, Magento, and custom-built sites. We adapt our technical recommendations to each platform's capabilities.
How often should a technical SEO audit be done?
We recommend a full audit quarterly, with monthly automated monitoring checks to catch new issues introduced by site updates, new content, or algorithm changes.
