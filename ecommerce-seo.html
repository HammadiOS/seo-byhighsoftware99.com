// FAQ

Ecommerce SEO Questions

Answers to common questions about SEO for online stores.

My store has 10,000 products — where do you start?
We start with a data-driven prioritization — identifying your highest-revenue, highest-potential products and optimizing those first for maximum ROI before scaling to the full catalogue.
Do you work with Shopify stores?
Yes. Shopify is one of our primary platforms. We work around Shopify's known SEO limitations (forced URL structures, duplicate content) and maximize results within the platform's capabilities.
Can SEO compete with Google Shopping ads?
Absolutely. Organic product rankings convert at 4x higher rates than paid ads and have zero per-click cost. SEO builds a compounding traffic asset; ads stop the moment you stop paying.
How do you handle out-of-stock or discontinued products?
We implement proper 301 redirects for discontinued products and handle temporarily out-of-stock pages carefully to retain their ranking value while communicating availability.
Do you write product descriptions for every item?
For large catalogues, we prioritize and batch-write descriptions for your top revenue products first, then systematically expand coverage. We never use AI-generated content without thorough human editing.
