// FAQ

YouTube SEO Questions

Common questions about growing on YouTube through SEO.

Can you optimize videos that are already uploaded?
Yes. We can retroactively optimize existing videos with better titles, descriptions, tags, thumbnails, and transcripts — often producing significant ranking improvements on existing content.
How many subscribers do I need before YouTube SEO works?
Zero. YouTube SEO works at any channel size. In fact, small channels often benefit most from SEO because they rely on search discovery rather than algorithmic recommendations that favor established channels.
Do you help with video script or production?
We provide SEO-optimized video briefs and script outlines that incorporate target keywords naturally. Full production is separate, but we guide the content structure for maximum retention.
How long does it take for YouTube SEO to show results?
Newly uploaded optimized videos can begin ranking within days for lower-competition keywords. Channel-wide improvements typically compound over 2–4 months as YouTube's algorithm learns your content's value.
Do you help videos rank on Google as well as YouTube?
Yes. We specifically target keywords where Google shows video results, giving your optimized videos the opportunity to rank in both YouTube search and Google's video carousel simultaneously.
